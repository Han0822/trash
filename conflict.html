Conflict

key problem:

Public doesn't see the ugly side of recycling. Media think their trash is took care by fancy machine and advanced technology. But in reality , Trash is sorting by poor children without any protection.

Different material is sent to different direction. For PET, like water bottle mostly will be recycled in Shanghai city. Their factories have very advanced high technology, and high demand for PET to make fabric. Plastic bag will be sent to Shandong and Fujian province where they inspect every piece .If it is clear and clean, worker will cut the label, and chop it up, then sell it to make new plastic bag.

The most controversial is the third direction, the red lines, of the mixed trashes. It contain all kind of trash, like rock, dirt, glass, paper or chemistry. It will be sent to Hebei, Guangzhou and Fujian provinces, where worker dump it into the sink, and scooped out the plastic that floats. The bottle of the sink will end up in landfill or river. Some of the trash will be sorted by children.

Trashes were duped into ponds contaminating water.

Mothers feed their baby in the field.

Children work without any protection or training.

Most recycler are women and children, because most men are left village to find better jobs in the city.
